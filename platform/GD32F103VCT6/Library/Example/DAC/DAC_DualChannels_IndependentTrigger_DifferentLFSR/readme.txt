  @brief
    This demo is based on the GD32107C-EVAL board,in this demo,use DAC Dual channels
  to generate a signal with noise waves with DifferentLFSR on DAC Dual channels output.
  DAC Dual channels conversion are configured to be triggered by TIMRE2 TRGO and by 
  TIMRE3 TRGO.
